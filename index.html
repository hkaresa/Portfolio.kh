About

I'm Karesa.

I build things to understand them. Theses, models, a trading agent that runs on its own — if I can't make an idea work in practice, I don't fully trust that I understand it.

I'm finishing my MSc in Finance at Imperial. Before that, UC Irvine, where I led the equity team at the student investment fund and co-founded a Scholars of Finance chapter. Somewhere in the middle, six months and ten countries on my own — which quietly rearranged what I want this work to be about: less the title, more the judgment.

These days I split my time between two ways of reading a market — the fundamental analyst's and the systematic engineer's — and I'm looking for asset management and global macro roles where both count.
