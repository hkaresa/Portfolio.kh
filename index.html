About

I'm Karesa.

I'm currently finishing my MSc in Finance & Accounting at Imperial. Before that, UC Irvine, where I led the equity team at the student investment fund, co-founded a Scholars of Finance chapter, used my platform as a Peer Academic Advisor to advocate for transfer students, lobbied on their behalf at the California State Senate, and launched events and initiatives for the transfer community. Then, between UCI and Imperial, six months and ten countries on my own — the stretch that taught me the most about myself, and about handling the unfamiliar.

These days I split my time between understanding the macroeconomic environment and its connections and implications across the global economy, building with Claude, reading, traveling, working on my master's thesis, and exploring more of London.
